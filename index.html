November 12, 2025

UP in IT Cebu, or the UP Cebu Information Technology Center, is the unit that plans, builds, and maintains the campus' IT environment. It oversees the local area network, internet connectivity, servers, and computer laboratories used for teaching, research, and administration, helping ensure that UP Cebu's academic programs in IT and Computer Science have reliable technical support and infrastructure.

DYNATA

November 13, 2025

Dynata Cebu is the Cebu-based office of Dynata, a global market research and data company that operates large online panels of respondents who answer surveys for brands, agencies, and researchers. The Cebu site mainly serves as an operations and support hub, with teams handling project coordination, survey and data operations, customer support, and account services that feed into Dynata's worldwide insights business.

RIVANT IT CEBU

November 13, 2025

Rivant IT Cebu is a Philippines-based IT training center that delivers hands-on bootcamps in high-demand technologies such as networking, VoIP, fiber optics, and Linux using real industry equipment. It offers beginner to advanced courses led by experienced instructors, maintains strong certification exam pass rates, and focuses on preparing learners for both local and overseas IT careers.

MATA

November 14, 2025

MATA in Cebu usually refers to Mata Technologies Inc., a homegrown tech company based in Cebu City that specializes in immersive digital experiences, especially 360° virtual tours and virtual reality maps for real estate and tourism in the Philippines. The company creates VR maps, 3D/360 tours, and 360° photography (indoor, outdoor, aerial) and has partnered with tourism offices and other organizations to showcase destinations and properties online.
